Our Story of Empowering Youth

Transforming lives through education, support, and opportunity since 2018

Our Vision

A world where all young people have equal opportunities to thrive, regardless of their background or circumstances.

Our Mission

To empower disadvantaged youth through comprehensive education, skills development, and support services that break cycles of poverty and gender-based violence.

Our Approach

Holistic programs addressing education, health, economic empowerment, and social support to create lasting change.

Our Journey

Milestones in our story of impact

2018

Foundation Established

Founded by a group of youth activists to address educational disparities in Nairobi's informal settlements.

2018

First Skills Center Opened

Launched our flagship vocational training program serving 50 youth annually.

2019

National Expansion

Expanded operations to 3 additional counties with focus on rural youth empowerment.

2021

GBV Support Program

Added comprehensive gender-based violence prevention and survivor support services.

2023

10,000 Youth Impacted

Reached our milestone of transforming 10,000 young lives across Kenya.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide our work

Inclusion

We serve all youth regardless of gender, ethnicity, religion, or socioeconomic status.

Innovation

We continuously develop creative solutions to youth challenges.

Integrity

We maintain transparency and accountability in all operations.

Compassion

We approach every youth with empathy and understanding.

Impact

We measure success by tangible improvements in youth lives.

Collaboration

We partner with communities for sustainable change.
