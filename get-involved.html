Ways to Engage

There are many ways to support our mission

Volunteer With Us

Our volunteers are the backbone of our organization. Whether you can give a few hours a week or lead a long-term project, your time and skills can make a real difference.

Volunteer Opportunities:

  • Program Facilitator: Lead workshops in your area of expertise
  • Mentor: Guide youth through our various programs
  • Event Volunteer: Help with fundraising and awareness events
  • Professional Services: Offer pro bono services (legal, medical, etc.)
  • Community Outreach: Represent us in your community
Apply to Volunteer

Partnership Opportunities

We collaborate with businesses, schools, and other organizations to expand our impact and create meaningful change for youth in our communities.

Partnership Options:

  • Corporate Partnerships: Employee engagement, matching gifts, and sponsorship
  • School Partnerships: Bring our programs to your students
  • Community Organization Partnerships: Joint initiatives and referrals
  • In-Kind Donations: Contribute goods or services
  • Event Sponsorship: Support our fundraising events
Contact Our Partnerships Team

Become an Ambassador

Our Ambassadors help raise awareness and funds for our programs through their networks and communities.

Ambassador Roles:

  • Community Ambassador: Represent us in your local area
  • Campus Ambassador: Spread awareness on your school campus
  • Social Media Ambassador: Amplify our message online
  • Fundraising Ambassador: Organize local fundraising efforts

Benefits:

  • Leadership development
  • Networking opportunities
  • Volunteer recognition
  • Skill-building workshops
Join Our Ambassador Program

Internship Program

Gain valuable experience while contributing to our mission through our internship program.

Internship Areas:

  • Program Development: Help design and implement youth programs
  • Communications: Content creation, social media, and PR
  • Fundraising: Grant writing and event planning
  • Research: Data collection and impact assessment
  • Community Outreach: Partnership development and engagement

Program Details:

  • Minimum 3-month commitment
  • 10-20 hours per week
  • Academic credit available
  • Remote and in-person options
